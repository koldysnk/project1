Commission Request

If you are interested in a commissioned piece, please email Grace Barnes or fill out one of the below forms. Please include the dimensions of the wanted work, subject matter, color scheme, and medium, such as graphite, acrylic paint, etc. If you would like a recreation of a picture or specific object, please also attach an image of it. If you have a concept in mind but are unsure of exactly how you want it to look, please contact me and we can work together to come up with an idea that satisfies your needs.
