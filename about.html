About

Artist's Statement

As an artist I have always been inspired by the human figure. The human form can be found throughout most, if not all, of my art. I work primarily with acrylic paint, but have experimented with all types of medium; graphic design, illustration, printmaking, 3D design, and jewelry making. The body of work you see is a breadth collection, not a part of a concentration, but they are connected through their focus on the human form.
